ABOUT DATA SPRINT

> DATA SPRINT 3.0 is a 24-hour data-centric innovation challenge by the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science.


> A competitive arena where innovators and creators push boundaries to solve practical problems through artificial intelligence and data-driven solutions.


> Step into an intense 24-hour journey of innovation, teamwork and breakthrough ideas.

RULES & GUIDELINES

> Each team must consist of exactly 4 members.

> Participants must bring their own laptop and an Ethernet cable.

> Proper dress code must be followed throughout the event.

> Spot registration is not permitted.

> Preliminary rounds will be conducted, and only shortlisted teams will proceed further.

> The hackathon will consist of 4 rounds, with cumulative evaluation for winner announcement.

> Certificates will be provided to all participants.

> The venue for the event will be announced soon.

> The judges’ and organizers’ decisions will be final and binding.

FAQ

> Who can participate?
Students from any undergraduate or graduate program interested in AI and Data Science.
> Team size?
Strictly 4 members per team.
> Registration fee?
The hackathon is completely free for all selected teams.
> Will lunch be provided?
Yes. Lunch will be provided to all the participants.
> What is the reporting time?
Details will be shared with the selected participants via email.
> How many events can a participant participate in?
Participants can engage in multiple technical events throughout the hackathon duration.
> Will there be any certificates?
Yes, all participants will receive participation certificates, and winners will receive merit certificates.
> Is Guardian allowed?
No, guardians are not allowed inside the hackathon premises.
> Will OD recommendation letters be provided?
Yes, On-Duty (OD) recommendation letters will be provided to all participants.
> Is there a dress code?
Formal or business casual attire is recommended.
> Do I need to bring my own laptop?
Yes, all participants must bring their own laptops and necessary peripherals.
> Is On-Spot Registration Available?
No, only pre-registered and selected teams can participate.
